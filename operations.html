Our Story

From Wholesale Pioneer to Singapore's Luggage Partner

Founded in 1979 as Sin Kwang Wah, we started as a traditional bags and luggage wholesaler in Singapore's industrial heartland. Over 47 years, we've built an extensive network of trusted manufacturers globally and evolved into Luggage Outlet — serving everyone from individual travellers to Fortune 500 corporations.

Our belief is simple: quality does not have to come with a hefty price tag. By sourcing directly from manufacturers and operating from our Yishun Industrial Park warehouse, we cut out the middleman and pass the savings to you.

Today we serve government agencies (MINDEF, MHA, MOE), multinationals (ExxonMobil, Google, Singtel, Shell), and thousands of individual customers — delivering over 7,000 customised units for a single corporate order.

Quality Assurance

Every Bag Checked Before It Leaves

🔍 Incoming Inspection

Every shipment from manufacturers undergoes spot-check inspection — zippers, wheels, handles, fabric integrity, stitching quality. Defective units rejected before entering inventory.

🎨 Branding Quality Check

After customisation (silkscreen, embroidery, engraving), each unit is inspected for logo accuracy, colour matching, alignment, and durability. Sample approved before bulk run.

📋 Pre-Delivery Audit

Final count verification, packaging integrity check, and branded insert cards added before dispatch. Corporate orders get photo documentation of completed batches.

🛡️ Repair & Support

Wheel replacements, zipper repairs, and handle fixes available at our Yishun showroom. Manufacturer warranty on selected products — check with us for details.
